### Data which is not part of the repository and needs to 
### be downloaded to folder "Data":

  ## Population counts of UN World Population Prospects 2019:
  
    1. WPP2019_INT_F03_3_POPULATION_BY_AGE_ANNUAL_FEMALE.xlsx
    2. WPP2019_INT_F03_2_POPULATION_BY_AGE_ANNUAL_MALE.xlsx
    
    URL: https://population.un.org/wpp/Download/Standard/Population/

    1. time_series_covid19_confirmed_global.csv
    2. time_series_covid19_deaths_global.csv
    
    URL: https://data.humdata.org/dataset/novel-coronavirus-2019-ncov-cases
  
  ## Abridged life tables of UN World Population Prospects 2019:
  
    1. WPP2019_MORT_F17_1_ABRIDGED_LIFE_TABLE_BOTH_SEXES.xlsx
    2. WPP2019_MORT_F17_3_ABRIDGED_LIFE_TABLE_FEMALE.xlsx 
    3. WPP2019_MORT_F17_2_ABRIDGED_LIFE_TABLE_MALE.xlsx
    
    URL: https://population.un.org/wpp/Download/Standard/Mortality/
